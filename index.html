How to Play
ALIBI is a true detective deduction game. You must solve murder mysteries by analyzing suspect alibis and finding the fatal flaws hidden in their stories.
You are dropped into an open-ended scenario. Chat freely with the suspects using the AI interface. Uncover their hidden secrets to break their alibis. Once you've tracked their exact timelines, click "Conclude" to name the killer and submit your proven theory on their true motive.
